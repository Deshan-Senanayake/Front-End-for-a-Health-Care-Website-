What is myHealth.com?

Your Medical Diagnosis and Health Record keeper


We help users in self-diagnosis once the health-related information/ symptoms/ health reports are entered to the website. It can indicate the health issue, severity, and the potential health hazards they might suffer in the future. Based on the severity of the condition, it can suggest remedies to users or advise them to go for a checkup. It can suggest the schedules of the specialist doctors in the domain who visit to the nearest hospitals/medical centres. The users can also purchase various medical equipment to help in treatment and diagnosis via our web site.
